How to Layer Fragrances for Lasting Scent

01

Start with Body Wash

Shower and exfoliate to remove dead skin cells, allowing the fragrance to adhere better.

02

Apply Body Cream

Apply a fragrance-free or matching scented lotion to lock in moisture and create a smooth canvas.

03

Mist and Set

Start with a body spray, layer with a body oil for depth, and finish with perfume on pulse points.

04

Touch Up as Needed

Reapply light layers throughout the day to keep your fragrance fresh and lasting.
